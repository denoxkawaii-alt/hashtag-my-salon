#About

Not just a salon.
A whole grooming ecosystem.

Hashtag My Salon is built as four experiences under one roof: a unisex styling salon, a relaxation spa, a certified hair & beauty academy, and a dedicated hair transplantation clinic. Black-and-gold interiors, well-trained stylists, and a space designed to feel unhurried — that's the reputation we've built, one appointment at a time.
